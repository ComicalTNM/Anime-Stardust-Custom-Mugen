This is my first public release character.

Although it is just an edit, I have spent a lot of time to do it, 
including adding new moves, new mechanics, new interactions, new effects, 
writing strong AI, etc. Not to mention that debugging is another difficult part, 
as tons of testing may needed just to find out and solve a small problem.

In order to ensure everything is ok with no bugs, I have tested my Dio to fight 
with many other characters. During this process, I further improved the AI, 
making him smart enough to defeat many other characters.

This Dio is really like the real DIO. You can abuse his ZA WARUDO timestop to 
teleport and dodge incoming attacks, or to stop the time and that of the enemies 
to unleash his deadly moves, like throwing lots of knives and the MUDA MUDA MUDA. 
Of course, the ROAD ROLLER Ultimate is always the perfect option to finish off 
your opponent with no mercy.

Somehow this Dio does not forget his ability as a vampire. 
Space Ripper Stingy Eyes makes him able to attack his targets from distance. 
Vaporization Freezing Technique can freeze and reduce the Power of his opponent. 
The HP recovered by Blood Sucking can be the key to turn defeat into victory. 
By the way, this Dio does not believe in gravity, so he can fly(forward) during the battle.

About the new mechanics of both characters being able to enter the timestop at 
the same time, sadly it cannot be applied to all the Dio and Jotaro made by other 
creators in a general way. 
(Even if that works, many other Dio and Jotaro are unhittable during their timestop, 
thus it is hard to fight against with them even if you can move in the timestop.) 
So up to now, I guess only my Dio can react and enter the timestop when his opponent/partner 
is yelling Za Warudo.
Similarly, for the interacting ROAD ROLLER Ultimate, it needs a specific state and 
new animation to make the scene where both sides are hitting the road roller be achieved. 
Therefore, again, I guess only my Dio can do this interaction with the Road Roller up to now.

Anyway, if you are reading my words up to this point, I really appreciate that 
and thank you so much for spending your time here.
Hope you like my Dio. Enjoy XD


P.S. If you think the damage is too much, feel free to modify the "attack" parameter 
under [Data] in DIO_K.cns. (Default value is 100)
