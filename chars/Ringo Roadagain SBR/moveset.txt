中文：
A 四段
B 一段
C 射击
下C 对空射击
跳A 一段
跳B 快速落地
跳C 对地射
Y 潮流防御/取消
Z 冲刺（有CD）
下前A 连续射击
下后A 当身，当当身触发时按下A可以触发连续射击
下前B 丢枪，第二段瞬移到枪的位置
下后B 场上有枪时使用可以脱离
下前C 倒流时间，打断对手状态并强制攻击对手
下后C 残血限定技，下前B丢枪记录当前时刻，按下下后C对手和玩家均回到该时刻的状态（能量不会恢复防止无限回溯）
X 超杀 当身

English:
C/down C/air C gunshot to different directions
air B fast landing
Y stylish evade/cancel
Z Dash (has a CD)
DFA multiple shoots
DBA counter, when it is activated press down A to trigger a multiple shoot
DFB drop ur gun, use the skill again to teleport back to ur gun
DBB when there's a gun, you can escape combo and teleport back to ur gun by this skill
DFC Time reverse, force the opponent to stop and then attack the opponent
DBC Only usable when ur in low health mode, when there's a gun, reverse the time to the moment you dropped this gun. (But u and ur opponent's energy bar won't be restored in case that people spam the skill)
X Ultimate, counter
