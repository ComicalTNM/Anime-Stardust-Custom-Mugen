Mechanics:
Special cancel:
you can cancel specials in other specials, quite simple, the only rule is that you can't cancel a special in the same special

Counter hit:
When you hit an enemy that was attacking you get a counter hit (very common in fighting games), double the damage, hitstun on some hits,
 on other attacks it increases the juggle, etc.

counter hit cancel whit specials:
If you cancel an attack that counter hit a special with very good timing, this special will cost no energy.

counter hit starter:
when you start a combo with a counter hit, the first special of your combo will split its cost in half

first attack:
when you land the first hit in a round multiplies by 3 
the damage of the attack hit (multiplies by 4 to 
the weakest attacks)

first counter attack:
is the combination of the counter hit and the first attack, multiplies by 3 
and then multiplies by 2 the damage of the successful hit


head shot mechanic:
In mugen all characters have a headshot distance value, when you are close to that distance and land an aerial hit, you will get a headshot


head counter hit:
the combination of the head shot and the counter hit

first head attack:
the combination of the head shot and the first attack

first head counter attack:
the combination of the head shot and first counter attack

dodge guard cancel:(-500)

you can dodge with a+b , a+b ,foward a+b and back a+b, and you can also do it while getting up or while blocking an attack but cost 500 of power,
you can also do it in the air

quick time event: a+x

extra quick time event very similar to rage of the dragon by EVOGA, but in this case , the button sequence is 
random, it does a lot of damage, but you are left exposed and you have to be precise

Tech normal grab:

the normal grab that is done with y , you can get rid of it with a+b , b+c , a+c ,x+y ,y+z,x+z , 
but only with good timing in the first few seconds in the one that catches you

you can't break free from the first grab you get in a combo

if you do a counter hit with the grab,the seconds you have to break free are halved, you will have less 
time to be precise, plus the grab will do double damage on the counter hit

Taunt mechanic:
when you perform a taunt with the z command while your enemy is lying on the ground, you will be able to remove 1000 energy, 
you can only do this once per round, the rest of the times you try it you will remove very little energy, and that loss is almost unnoticeable


Blood mechanic:
chainsaw man when hitting will leave puddles of blood sometimes, when you crouch near one of these, you will recover life faster
