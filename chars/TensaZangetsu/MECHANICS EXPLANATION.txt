- EXTREME CANCEL

Extreme Cancel means that all characters can now cancel to supers. It won't require the full 2000 energy cost, it will require 1500 instead,
but it will drain a little bit of your life in exchange, so be careful on how you use it!

(Extreme Cancel command will always be down, forward, down, forward, (buttons)

- ROLL GETUP

Simply press back/forward while lying down and the character will do a roll instead of just getting up, just like in Smash.

BREAK THE DEADLOCK! (COMBO BREAKER)

Press A+B+C while in a gethit state to break p2 combo and send him to the wall. Doesn't work on grabs/throws or supers. Needs 3000 power.

- COMBO STRING RESET

Press Z during a combo to do a string reset. Basically what this does is resetting the Reverse Beat mechanic (linking any basic attack 
to any basic) so you can do any basic after this and extend your combo even further. Works only on ground and needs 1500 power.

- PUSH BLOCK

Just like in MVC2 and others, press A+B or B+C while guarding to push p2 away and escape. Needs 1000 power.

- JUST DEFEND

Like in Garou MOTW, press back at the exact time p2 attack is going to hit you to perform a Just Defend. Just Defending succesfully
gives you the following benefits:

- Regain a small amount of health
- Increases your power gauge slightly
- Shorter blockstun
- No chip damage

To Just Defend multihit attacks, you need to press the forward button then back again for each hit, or then it won't work.
