Beerus God of Destruction - Moveset

A

Dash + A

Down + A

A + B (Beerus have Super Armor during this Move)

B

Dash + B

Down + B

C

Down + C

B + C (Hold) (you can physically move the energy balls by hit on them)

A Aire

Down + A Aire

B Aire

Down + B Aire

C Aire

Down + C Aire

B + C (And Aire) (Hold) (you can physically move the energy balls by hit on them)

Y - Super Dash

S - Power Charge

SPECIALS:

D,DF,F,a - Beerus's Dragon Kick (And Aire) (press back to change position before the last hit)

D,DB,B,a - God of Destruction's Strike

D,DF,F,b - God of Destruction's Wrath  (And Aire)  (Hold b to continue throw more beam)

D,DB,B,b - God of Destruction's Anger (And Aire) 

D,DF,F,c - Energy Hakai (And Aire) 

D,DB,B,c - Rampage Of Destruction (And Aire) 

SUPERS:

D,DF,F,a+b - Beerus Ball of Destruction (And Aire)
(if the enemy is in a good distance hold c so beerus will explode the ball with a Beam))

D,DF,F,b+c - Hakai
(Guard Break but not Much Distance)
(You can do this move only if the enemies don't have much life left)

ULTIMATE: 

D,DF,F,D,DF,F,a+b or x - Sphere Of Destruction (And Aire)
