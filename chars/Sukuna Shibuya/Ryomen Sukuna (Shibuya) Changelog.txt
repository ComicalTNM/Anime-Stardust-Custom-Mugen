Ryomen Sukuna (Shibuya) Changelog:

Version 2:

- Major Changes
	- New healing Signals for When Reverse Cursed Technique (RCT) is Active
	- New Defence UP Signals When Sukuna Stops Using RCT
	- New Defence DOWN Signals When Sukuna Starts Back Using RCT
	- Sukuna Now Reinforces Himself With Cursed Energy to Increase His Defense When Not Using RCT (.6 Increase)
	- Sukuna "Tempo" Gauge Changes
		- Fills Passively as Sukuna Either Deals Damage or is in Neutral
		- As the Gauge Fills Sukuna Becomes More Likely To Land a Black Flash
		- If a Black Flash is Landed the Gauge Resets
		- Gauge Drains Passively When Domain is Active
	- Black Flash Changes:
		- Landing a Black Flash Now Puts Sukuna Into "The Zone" 
		- Landing a Black Flash Restores Some of Sukuna's RCT
		- Landing a Black Flash Returns Sukuna's Domain Expansion if Already Used
	- Domain Expansion Changes:
		- Sukuna Can Now Use Either Maximum Domain or Regular Domain Depending on the Situation
		- Maximum Domain is 3x Stronger Than Regular Domain (3x Damage & Health)
		- Maximum Domain Does Not Deal Damage While Sukuna Prepares Maximum Fuga
		- Sukuna Will Not Use Maximum Fuga Upon Starting a Regular Domain
		- Regular Domain Last 10 Seconds Longer Than Maximum Domain (30 Seconds Total)
		- Domain Expansion Nw Puts Sukuna Into "The Zone"
	- The Zone:
		- When Active Sukuna Gains a Change To Dodge Attacks For a Brief Moment
		- Sukuna Gains Access to a Full Screen Rampage Attack

- AI Overhaul:
	- Improved Neutral AI
	- Domain Expansion is Now Used Based On Different Condition's 
	Ex: 
	- Boredom | The longer the Match Goes, Sukuna Will Grow Bored of his Opponent and Aim to Finish them Quickly
	- Caution | If the Enemy Heals Enough Suddenly, Sukuna Will Try to Stop Them in Their Tracks
	- Desperation | If Sukuna Health Gets too low Sukuna Will Try to Regain Control of the Battle
