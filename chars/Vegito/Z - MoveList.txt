• X+A: Dragon Rush (And Aire) (Can Break Guard)
• X+B: Vanish Wallbounce (And Aire)

[Dodge, Counter, Reflect, Reset Combo, Combo Break System]

• Z - Dogde
• FordWard + Z - Dogde FordWard
• Back + Z - Dogde Back (Can Do it During Guard, it Take More Power)

• Aire Z - Dogde

• Down + Z - Counter
• Down + Z - During Combo (To Extend your Combo)

• Z+A - Deflect (In Low And Aire) (Can Do it During Guard, it Take More Power)
• Z+B - Break the Deadlock (Release it diring your Hurt Animation) (Take all the Power)

[Power Charge, Taunts]

• S (Start) - Power Charge

• Down + S - Taunts (Press Down + S+A or Down + S+B to take  a Specifique Taunt) 
(If You taunt him while he's lying down, you'll take one power bar from Him)	

[SPECIALS]

Down, Forward + A - Rampaging Rush (And Aire)

Down, Backwards + A - Pressure Sign (And Aire) (on the ground Press A to Attack Directly) 

Down, Forward + B - Atomic Rush (And Aire)

Down, Backwards + B - Explosive Wave (And Aire)

Down, Forward + C - Kamehameha (Hold) (And Aire)
(Hold down to Release towards the ground) 

Down, Backwards + C - Impact Meteor (And Aire) (Can Break Guard) (Hold the Opposite Direction or Down Before the Throw)

[SUPERS ATTACKS]

Down, Forward + A+B - Big Bang Cannon (And Aire)

[ULTIMATE] 

Down, Forward, Down, Forward + A+B or Z + C - Atomic Strike (And Aire)

[Transformation]

X + C - Vegito (Super Saiyan)

Down + X+C - Vegito (Super Saiyan Blue)
